## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

- Username

- Password (stored as a secure hash, never in plain text)

### 1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Service:

- Pages visited and features used
- Browser type and version
- Device type and screen resolution
- Referring URL
- Session duration and interaction patterns

This data is collected through Google Analytics to help us understand how users interact with our platform and improve the Service.

### 1.3 User Content

We store the content you upload and create on the platform, including:

- Audio samples and sound files
- Kit configurations and settings
- Kit metadata (names, descriptions, categories)

### 1.4 Technical Data

Our infrastructure provider Cloudflare automatically collects:

- IP address (for security and performance purposes)
- Request headers and metadata
- Connection information

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
- Process your account registration and manage your account
- Store and serve your uploaded content
- Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience (via Google Analytics)
- Protect against unauthorized access and abuse (via Cloudflare security and rate limiting)
- Send important service-related notifications
- Enforce our Terms of Service

## 3. Data Storage and Infrastructure

### 3.1 Cloudflare Services

Your data is processed and stored using Cloudflare's infrastructure:

- **Cloudflare D1** — Our primary database for account information, kit metadata, and application data
- **Cloudflare R2** — Object storage for audio samples and uploaded files
- **Cloudflare KV** — Key-value storage used for rate limiting and session management
- **Cloudflare Workers** — Serverless compute that processes your requests

All data is handled within Cloudflare's global network. Please refer to Cloudflare's privacy policy for details on their data handling practices.

### 3.2 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion:

**Step 1: Anonymization (Automatic)**

When you click Delete in your profile settings:

- Your account enters a **24-day grace period** with status "anonymization pending"
- After 24 days, your account is automatically anonymized:
  - Username becomes "User"
  - Avatar is removed
  - Login credentials are deleted (you cannot log in)
  - Your public kits and sounds remain visible
- You can **undo** anonymization within the 24-day grace period by contacting support

**Step 2: Complete Deletion (Requires Contact Form)**

For full deletion that removes all kits, sounds, and data permanently:

1. Click Delete in profile — you'll receive a deletion code via email
2. Visit our [Contact Form](/contact) and select "Account Deletion Request"
3. Enter your email and the deletion code
4. Agree to both checkboxes and submit
5. Complete data removal may take up to 30 days to process

Some anonymized data may be retained for analytical and legal purposes as required by law.

## 4. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect and analyze usage data. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to track user interactions. The data collected includes:

Cloudflare provides us with aggregated analytics about traffic patterns, security threats, and performance metrics. This data is generally anonymized and used for infrastructure monitoring.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
