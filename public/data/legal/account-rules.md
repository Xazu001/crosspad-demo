# Account Rules & Community Guidelines

Last updated: March 2, 2026

These rules apply to all Crosspad users. By creating an account, you agree to follow these guidelines. Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban.

**📖 [View all legal documents](/legal/all)**

## 1. Account Requirements

### 1.1 Registration

- You must provide a valid email address
- Your username must be between 3 and 30 characters
- Usernames may contain letters, numbers, and underscores only
- You may only create one account per person
- You must be at least 13 years old to create an account

_For legal account requirements, see [Terms of Service §3](/legal/terms#3-account-registration)._

### 1.2 Account Security

- You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account
- Use a strong, unique password that you do not use on other services
- Never share your login credentials with others
- Report any unauthorized access to your account immediately

### 1.3 Account Responsibility

- You are responsible for all activity that occurs under your account
- You may not transfer your account to another person
- You may not use your account for commercial purposes without authorization

## 2. Content Guidelines

### 2.1 Audio Samples

When uploading audio samples and creating kits, you must ensure that:

- You own the rights to all samples you upload, or have explicit permission to use them
- Samples do not contain copyrighted material (songs, recordings, etc.) without proper licensing
- Samples are not harmful, offensive, or inappropriate
- File sizes and formats comply with platform limits

_For legal content ownership, see [Terms of Service §4](/legal/terms#4-user-content)._

### 2.2 Kit Information

When creating and describing kits:

- Kit names and descriptions must be accurate and not misleading
- Do not use offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate language
- Do not impersonate other artists or brands in kit names
- Categories and tags must accurately reflect the kit's content

### 2.3 Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited:

- Copyrighted samples used without authorization
- Hateful, discriminatory, or violent content
- Sexually explicit or pornographic material
- Content that promotes illegal activities
- Spam or repetitive low-quality uploads
- Malicious files or content designed to exploit the platform

## 3. Community Behavior

### 3.1 Respect

- Treat all community members with respect
- Do not harass, bully, or intimidate other users
- Do not discriminate based on race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, or age

### 3.2 Fair Use

- Do not abuse the platform's resources (storage, bandwidth, API calls)
- Do not attempt to circumvent rate limits or usage restrictions
- Do not use automated tools to bulk-upload or scrape content
- Do not exploit bugs or vulnerabilities — report them instead

## 4. Intellectual Property

### 4.1 Your Content

- You retain ownership of original content you create and upload
- By sharing content publicly, you allow other users to listen to and use your kits within the platform
- You may request removal of your content at any time by deleting it

### 4.2 Others' Content

- Respect the intellectual property of other creators
- Do not copy, redistribute, or claim ownership of others' kits or samples
- Report content that you believe infringes on your rights

## 5. Enforcement

### 5.1 Violations

Crosspad reserves the right to take action against accounts that violate these rules:

- **First offense** — Warning and content removal
- **Repeated violations** — Temporary account suspension
- **Severe violations** — Permanent account ban and content deletion

### 5.2 Appeals

If you believe your account was actioned in error, you may contact us to request a review. We will evaluate appeals on a case-by-case basis.

## 6. Reporting

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these rules, please report it through the platform. All reports are reviewed and handled confidentially.
