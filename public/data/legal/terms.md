Account deletion is a two-step process:

- Your account enters a 24-day grace period (status: "anonymization pending")
- After 24 days, your account is automatically anonymized:

  - Login credentials are deleted

2. Submit a formal request via our [Contact Form](/contact) with:

   - Your email address
   - The deletion code from the email
   - Agreement to both checkboxes
3. Complete data removal may take up to 30 days
